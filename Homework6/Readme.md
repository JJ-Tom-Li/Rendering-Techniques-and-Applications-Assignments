# Homework6
Texture , Color blending , Anti-aliasing , Fog and Polygon offset.

## 操作方法 
+ 移動鏡頭: 
  - 滑鼠控制 
+ 機器手臂移動:
  * WSAD前後左右移動 
  * Space:Move arm up. 
  * X:Move arm down. 
  * 3:Move fingers. 
  - 4:Move thumb. 
+ 其他: 
  * Z:全螢幕
  * B:Snipe your target with blending. 
  * C:Trigger anti aliasing. 
  * F:Fog your scene. 
  * P:Offset your polygon. 
  - V:Show the bounding box.
